The approach

Dentistry built on evidence,not on brand names.

Twenty-one years of postgraduate training across orthodontics, restorative dentistry, implantology and sleep medicine — applied with a philosophy that puts the clinician's judgement, not the equipment, at the centre of your care.

Minimally invasive by default

Additive, tooth-preserving techniques wherever possible — restoring function without removing healthy structure.

Trained across disciplines

Orthodontics, restorative dentistry, implantology and dental sleep medicine, backed by postgraduate degrees and research.

The person, not the product

Aligner brands and appliances change. What matters is who is planning, monitoring and adjusting your treatment.

"The real difference isn't the logo on the box. It's the knowledge, experience, judgement and care of the clinician using it."

Dr Jelena Djordjevic

DDS · MSc · MFDS · PhD
Orthodontics Restorative & Aesthetic Dentistry Implantology Dental Sleep Medicine

Dr Jelena has spent 21 years across undergraduate teaching, postgraduate mentoring, multidisciplinary research and advanced clinical practice. She holds a Master's and PhD in Orthodontics, a Postgraduate Diploma in Restorative and Aesthetic Dentistry, an ITI Certificate in Implant Dentistry, and a Postgraduate Certificate in Dental Sleep Medicine.
